Why Pricemet?

Because we help you find great food at the price, YOU choose, not the price someone else chooses for you.

When YOU set your own price, you save up to 80% below the regular restaurant prices.

How it works

You choose the price

Choose where, how many people and how much you would like to spend.

We notify local restaurants

Your request is automatically sent to our network of restaurants.

You save time and money

Receive offers that best match your budget. Accept and purchase the vouchers right from your device.
